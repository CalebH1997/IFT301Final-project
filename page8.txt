      <img src="lance-reddick.jpg" alt="Lance Reddick">
      <p>Lance Reddick was born on December 31, 1962, in Baltimore, Maryland. He attended the Peabody Conservatory and the Yale School of Drama, where he earned his Master of Fine Arts degree in acting.</p>
      <p>Reddick has had a successful career in both television and film, with notable roles in shows like The Wire, Fringe, and Bosch, and films like John Wick and White House Down.</p>

      <h2>Destiny 2</h2>
      <p>Lance Reddick is perhaps best known to video game fans for his role as Commander Zavala in Destiny 2. Zavala is the leader of the Guardians and the head of the Vanguard, and he is one of the most beloved characters in the game.</p>
      <p>Reddick's performance as Zavala has been praised for its depth, intensity, and emotional range, and it has helped to make Destiny 2 one of the most popular games of all time.</p>
