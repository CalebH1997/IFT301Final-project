      <p>The story of Destiny 2: Shadowkeep revolves around the return of Eris Morn, a character from the original Destiny game who has been on a mission to uncover the secrets of the moon. Eris has discovered that the Nightmares are manifestations of the past, present, and future traumas of the Guardians, and they are being used by an ancient evil known as the Hive to gain power. To stop them, players must venture into the depths of the moon and confront their fears, as well as face new and returning enemies.</p>
